About Us

American Fleet was founded in 2013. The company provides innovative ideas for transforming American trucks into a reliable military-grade platform. We take into account any customer's wishes regarding vehicle modernzatin. We provide services for F-550 and F-600 trucks, including the modernization of wheels, tires, gears, fenders, axles and other tasks. People come to us when they need to modernize a truck for military tasks or everyday life. To deliver a quality result, we use parts that have been proven over the years and are installed by our best technicians.
